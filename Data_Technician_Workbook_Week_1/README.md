# Data Technician – Week 1
## Foundations of Data, Excel & Professional Communication

### 📅 Course Focus
This week covered:
- UK Data Laws & Regulations
- Excel data analysis functions
- Pivot Tables
- Data categorisation using SWITCH
- Business presentation skills

---

## 📚 Day 1 – Data Laws & Regulations

### Covered:
- Data Protection Act
- GDPR
- Freedom of Information Act
- Computer Misuse Act

### Key Learning:
- Importance of lawful data processing
- Data subject rights
- Legal consequences of data breaches
- Organisational accountability

---

## 📊 Day 2 – Excel Data Analysis

### Skills Practised:
- Creating Tables
- Sorting Data
- SUM() and AVERAGE() functions
- Conditional Formatting
- Data exploration
- Analytical thinking

---

## 📈 Day 3 – Pivot Tables & Data Categorisation

### Practical Tasks:
- Created Pivot Tables
- Analysed Bike Sales dataset
- Used `SWITCH()` function to categorise sales volume
- Identified data quality issues (e.g., spacing errors in country names)

---

## 🎤 Day 4 – Presenting Data to Senior Leaders

### Topics Covered:
- Structuring executive presentations
- Public speaking best practices
- Using visualisation tools (Excel, Power BI, Tableau)
- Explaining customer churn at 12-month renewal

---

## 💡 Key Skills Developed
- Legal compliance awareness
- Excel analysis fundamentals
- Data storytelling
- Executive-level communication
